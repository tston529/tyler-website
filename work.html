January 2018 - Present

Data Analyst (Internship)

For the Fall 2018 semester, I was tasked by the Kutztown University Foundation to analyze donor data and run predictive analyses on the data sets to create lists of potential high donors from a list of people affiliated with the university.
Most of the work went into scripting methods of cleaning and extracting 'better' data from the data sets; this was done with Python.
I used Weka to do the actual analysis, and put the predictions back into another Python script I wrote to rank the constituents and form a list based on the model's confidence and how highly the constituent was ranked.
